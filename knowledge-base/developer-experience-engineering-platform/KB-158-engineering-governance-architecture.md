## Executive Summary

Define the canonical Engineering Governance Architecture for DUKADESK.

The Enterprise Engineering Governance Platform shall provide a comprehensive governance framework ensuring that all engineering activities comply with enterprise architecture, security, quality, operational, compliance, AI, and business policies while enabling innovation, scalability, accountability, transparency, and continuous improvement.

Engineering governance shall operate as an enterprise capability embedded throughout the software lifecycle rather than as isolated approval activities.

## Purpose

Define how DUKADESK governs engineering standards, architectural compliance, engineering decisions, technical oversight, risk management, AI governance, and enterprise engineering accountability.

## Critical DUKADESK Architectural Rule

**All engineering policies, standards, architectural reviews, compliance validations, technical decisions, risk assessments, AI governance activities, exceptions, and engineering oversight within DUKADESK shall be governed exclusively through the canonical Enterprise Engineering Governance Architecture. No application, Builder Studio module, Marketplace extension, AI Builder Agent, engineering team, platform service, or organizational unit shall establish independent governance models, policy frameworks, review processes, or decision authorities outside the enterprise architecture, ensuring enterprise-wide consistency, accountability, auditability, AI readiness, and continuous engineering governance.**
