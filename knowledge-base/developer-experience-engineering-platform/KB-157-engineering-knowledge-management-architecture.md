## Executive Summary

Define the canonical Engineering Knowledge Management Architecture for DUKADESK.

The Enterprise Engineering Knowledge Platform shall provide a unified, AI-ready, governed architecture that transforms engineering knowledge into reusable enterprise assets, enabling continuous learning, informed decision-making, architectural consistency, operational resilience, and long-term organizational intelligence.

Engineering knowledge shall be treated as a strategic enterprise asset rather than an informal by-product of engineering activities.

## Purpose

Define how DUKADESK standardizes engineering knowledge management, institutional memory, architectural decision preservation, technical learning, operational experience capture, and enterprise knowledge reuse.

## Critical DUKADESK Architectural Rule

**All engineering knowledge, architectural decisions, technical standards, design patterns, lessons learned, operational experience, institutional memory, and engineering best practices within DUKADESK shall be created, governed, classified, preserved, and evolved exclusively through the canonical Enterprise Engineering Knowledge Management Architecture. No application, Builder Studio module, Marketplace extension, AI Builder Agent, engineering team, platform service, or organizational unit shall establish independent engineering knowledge repositories, decision records, or knowledge governance processes outside the enterprise architecture, ensuring a single source of truth, enterprise-wide traceability, AI readiness, organizational learning, and long-term engineering continuity.**
