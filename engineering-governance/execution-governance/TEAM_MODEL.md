# Team Model

This document defines the engineering team structure for DUKADESK OS.

## Team Types

| Team | Focus | Example |
|------|-------|---------|
| Platform Team | Shared infrastructure and services | Backend, Infrastructure |
| Product Team | User-facing capabilities | Mobile, Dashboards |
| Enabling Team | Tools, DevEx, and governance | Developer Experience |
| Operations Team | Platform operations and reliability | Platform Operations |

## Team Responsibilities

### Platform Team
- Build and maintain core services.
- Define platform APIs and contracts.
- Ensure scalability and security.

### Product Team
- Build user-facing features.
- Implement specifications end to end.
- Own user experience quality.

### Enabling Team
- Maintain engineering standards.
- Improve developer productivity.
- Provide tooling and templates.

### Operations Team
- Operate production services.
- Respond to incidents.
- Maintain observability and runbooks.

## Team Interaction

Teams collaborate through:

- Shared specifications
- Defined APIs and contracts
- Architecture board decisions
- Engineering work registry

## Team Size and Structure

Each team has:

- Team Lead
- Domain or Repository Owners
- Engineers
- AI agents as assigned

## Cross-Team Work

Cross-team initiatives are managed through:

- Epics that span multiple repositories
- Architecture board oversight
- Shared milestones and release trains
