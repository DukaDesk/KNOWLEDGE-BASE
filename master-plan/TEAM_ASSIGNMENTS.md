# Team Assignments

This document defines which human teams own which implementation areas.

## Parallel Teams

| Team | Area | Responsibilities |
|------|------|------------------|
| Team A | Infrastructure | Cloud infrastructure, networking, compute, storage |
| Team B | Identity | Authentication, authorization, user management |
| Team C | Runtime | Runtime engine, execution environment |
| Team D | Builder | Visual builder, design system, export pipeline |
| Team E | Backend | Backend core services, APIs, business logic |
| Team F | Mobile | Mobile applications |
| Team G | Website | Public website and marketing site |
| Team H | Dashboards | Business dashboard and tenant dashboard |
| Team I | Commerce | Commerce, payments, orders, media, notifications |
| Team J | AI | AI platform, analytics, integrations, automation |

## Cross-Team Dependencies

Teams depend on each other through:

- Shared APIs and SDKs
- Engineering specifications
- Release trains
- Architecture board decisions

## Team Responsibilities

Each team is accountable for:

- Delivering their assigned capabilities
- Maintaining their repositories
- Complying with governance standards
- Reporting progress and blockers
- Participating in cross-team coordination

## Scaling

As the organization grows, teams may split or new teams may form. Team assignments are reviewed quarterly.
