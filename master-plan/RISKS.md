# Risks

This document tracks known risks to the DUKADESK OS implementation.

## Risk Register

| ID | Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation | Owner |
|----|------|--------|------------|------------|-------|
| R-01 | Foundation dependencies delay downstream work | High | Medium | Buffer time, parallel work where possible | Program Lead |
| R-02 | Scope creep across waves | High | Medium | Strict specification governance, change control | Architecture Board |
| R-03 | Talent or team capacity constraints | Medium | Medium | Clear priorities, AI agent support, hiring plan | Engineering Lead |
| R-04 | AI agent errors or quality issues | Medium | Medium | Role separation, human review, verification gates | Quality Lead |
| R-05 | Security vulnerabilities in new code | High | Low | Security reviews, scanning, secure defaults | Security Lead |
| R-06 | Integration complexity between repositories | High | Medium | Dependency graph, contract tests, staged integration | Integration Lead |
| R-07 | Performance bottlenecks at scale | High | Low | Performance testing in Wave 6, capacity planning | Platform Operations |
| R-08 | Compliance or regulatory issues | High | Low | Compliance review, legal input, audit trails | Compliance Lead |
| R-09 | Tooling or CI/CD instability | Medium | Medium | Redundant tooling, monitoring, runbooks | DevEx Lead |
| R-10 | Communication gaps between teams | Medium | Medium | Master plan reviews, shared specifications, sync meetings | Program Lead |

## Risk Management Process

1. Identify risks during planning and reviews.
2. Assess impact and likelihood.
3. Define mitigation and owner.
4. Monitor risks weekly during active waves.
5. Escalate risks that threaten milestones.

## Contingency Plans

High-impact risks have contingency plans documented and reviewed before wave start.

## Updates

This register is updated continuously. New risks are added and existing risks are re-evaluated during program reviews.
