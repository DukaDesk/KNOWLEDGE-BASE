## 1. Purpose

This document defines how DUKADESK generates revenue and how value flows between the platform, customers, and marketplace creators.

---

## 2. Revenue Streams

| Stream | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Edition Subscriptions** | Recurring revenue from SaaS and dedicated deployments |
| **Usage-Based Fees** | Charges for resources consumed beyond edition limits |
| **Marketplace Commissions** | Revenue share on paid marketplace extensions |
| **Professional Services** | Implementation, customization, and training services |
| **Support Tiers** | Premium support packages |
| **Enterprise Contracts** | Custom agreements for large organizations |

---

## 3. Pricing Dimensions

| Dimension | How It Works |
|-----------|--------------|
| **Edition** | Base price determined by tier |
| **Users** | Per-seat pricing within edition limits |
| **Tenants** | Additional cost for multi-tenant deployments |
| **Resources** | Storage, bandwidth, compute, API calls |
| **Capabilities** | Premium capabilities may carry add-on fees |
| **Marketplace** | Creator-set pricing with platform revenue share |

---

## 4. Marketplace Revenue Share

| Extension Type | Platform Share | Creator Share |
|----------------|----------------|---------------|
| Apps | 20% | 80% |
| Themes | 20% | 80% |
| Components | 20% | 80% |
| Templates | 20% | 80% |
| Actions | 20% | 80% |
| Workflows | 20% | 80% |
| AI Agents | 25% | 75% |

---

## 5. Customer Billing

Customers are billed:

- Monthly or annually for subscriptions
- In arrears for usage-based charges
- At point of purchase for marketplace extensions

---

## 6. Monetization Principles

1. Pricing is transparent and predictable.
2. Customers pay for value, not complexity.
3. Marketplace creators retain the majority of revenue.
4. Free tiers enable adoption without friction.
5. Enterprise pricing is customized to contract terms.
