## 1. What Is DUKADESK?

DUKADESK is a **composable, server-driven, multi-tenant business platform**.

It enables organizations to design, deploy, and operate tailored digital applications without building custom software from scratch.

Organizations compose their platform by selecting capabilities, configuring them to match their business, and extending them through the marketplace.

---

## 2. Core Value Proposition

> **Every business gets the software it needs — configured, not coded.**

DUKADESK delivers:

- **Speed:** Launch applications in days or weeks, not months.
- **Flexibility:** Adapt workflows, screens, and data models without engineering.
- **Scale:** Multi-tenant infrastructure serves organizations of any size.
- **Ecosystem:** Marketplace extends the platform with components, apps, themes, and integrations.
- **Longevity:** Architecture-first design protects investment over decades.

---

## 3. Platform Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Builder Studio** | Visual environment for designing applications, workflows, and data models |
| **Runtime Engine** | Server-driven execution environment that renders applications across surfaces |
| **Marketplace** | Distribution platform for apps, themes, components, templates, and integrations |
| **Platform Services** | Shared services for identity, data, integration, messaging, AI, and operations |

---

## 5. Platform Principles

1. **Composable by design** — capabilities can be selected, configured, and combined.
2. **Server-driven** — UI and behavior are defined as structured data, not hardcoded clients.
3. **Multi-tenant first** — every capability is built for independent organizations on shared infrastructure.
4. **Extensible** — third parties can extend the platform through well-defined extension points.
5. **Cloud-agnostic** — deployable across cloud providers and on-premises.
6. **Offline-capable** — core functionality works without continuous connectivity.
