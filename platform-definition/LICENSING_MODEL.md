## 1. Purpose

This document defines the licensing terms, restrictions, and entitlements for DUKADESK editions and marketplace extensions.

---

## 2. Platform License

The DUKADESK platform is licensed to customers on a subscription basis.

| License Type | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| **SaaS Subscription** | Cloud-hosted access to a specific edition |
| **Dedicated License** | Dedicated deployment of the platform |
| **Embedded License** | Use of the SDK to build applications on the platform |
| **OEM License** | White-label use of DUKADESK under another brand |

---

## 3. License Entitlements

Every license grants:

- Access to the licensed edition's capabilities and features
- A defined number of users, tenants, and resources
- Support level associated with the edition
- Marketplace access level
- Right to receive updates and security patches

---

## 4. License Restrictions

Unless explicitly permitted:

- Customers may not reverse engineer the platform.
- Customers may not resell platform access without an OEM agreement.
- Customers may not exceed licensed usage limits.
- Customers may not remove branding except under white-label terms.

---

## 5. Marketplace License

Marketplace extensions are licensed separately by their creators.

| Marketplace License | Description |
|---------------------|-------------|
| **Free** | No cost, may have usage limits |
| **Paid Subscription** | Recurring fee |
| **Per-Use** | Fee per transaction or invocation |
| **Per-Seat** | Fee per user |
| **Per-Tenant** | Fee per tenant |

---

## 6. Compliance

License compliance is enforced through:

- Usage metering
- Feature gating
- Audit logs
- Periodic license audits

Violations may result in feature restrictions or account suspension.
