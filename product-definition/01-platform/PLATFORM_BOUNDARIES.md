# Platform Boundaries

## What Is Part of DUKADESK

- Building and publishing tenant apps
- Managing users, roles, and permissions
- Content and media management
- Commerce, orders, and payments
- Notifications and communication
- Admin dashboards for tenant owners
- Marketplace for templates and extensions
- Web and mobile app delivery
- Analytics and reporting
- AI-assisted design and automation

## What Is Not Part of DUKADESK

- General-purpose cloud infrastructure outside the platform
- Hardware or IoT device management
- Accounting software (integrations may exist)
- Payroll or HR systems (integrations may exist)
- Physical logistics or shipping operations
- Banking beyond payment processing
- Social networking between unrelated tenants

## Out of Scope for Now

| Item | Reason | Future Consideration |
|------|--------|----------------------|
| Multi-tenant social features | Not core value | Possible marketplace enhancement |
| Native iOS/Android custom code | No-code first | May allow simple code later |
| White-label native mobile apps | Lower priority | Enterprise edition option |
| Offline-first full functionality | Complex | Roadmap item |

## Boundary Principles

1. DUKADESK owns the app platform, not the organization’s entire tech stack.
2. Integrations extend DUKADESK but do not replace core modules.
3. The marketplace offers extensions, but the platform remains usable without it.
4. Each tenant has one app. Multi-app tenants are out of scope.
