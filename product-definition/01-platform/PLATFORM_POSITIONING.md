# Platform Positioning

## Market Position

DUKADESK sits between simple website builders and custom enterprise platforms. It gives non-technical organizations the power to build a real app with users, content, commerce, and communication — without hiring a development team.

## Target Audience

### Primary

Small and medium organizations that need a digital app but lack technical resources.

Examples:

- Churches wanting a member app
- Schools wanting a parent/student portal
- Local stores wanting online ordering
- Service businesses wanting bookings and payments

### Secondary

- Developers and agencies building apps for clients using DUKADESK as a foundation.
- Designers creating templates and themes for the marketplace.

## Value Proposition

> Build and run your organization’s app in one place.

DUKADESK provides everything needed to go from idea to live app:

- Visual design tools
- Built-in business modules
- User and role management
- Commerce and payments
- Public app and admin dashboards
- Marketplace for templates and extensions

## Differentiation

| Alternative | Limitation | DUKADESK Advantage |
|-------------|------------|--------------------|
| Website builders | Static or simple pages | Full app with users, data, and commerce |
| App builders | Generic templates | Tenant-specific modules and journeys |
| E-commerce platforms | Only selling | Flexible modules for any organization type |
| Custom development | Expensive and slow | No-code with extensibility |

## Positioning Statement

For small and medium organizations that need a digital presence, DUKADESK is a no-code app platform that lets them build, publish, and operate a custom app for their audience — unlike website builders or e-commerce tools, DUKADESK combines design, business modules, user management, and commerce in one integrated platform.
