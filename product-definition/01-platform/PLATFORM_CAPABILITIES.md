# Platform Capabilities

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Visual App Building

Users can design their tenant app using a visual builder. The builder supports pages, components, layouts, themes, and navigation.

### 2. User Identity and Access

Organizations can define users, roles, and permissions. Users can authenticate through the app and access content based on their role.

### 3. Content Management

Tenants can create and manage content such as pages, posts, media, announcements, and resources.

### 4. Commerce

Tenants can sell products or services, accept payments, manage orders, and issue invoices.

### 5. Communication

Tenants can communicate with their audience through notifications, messages, announcements, and email.

### 6. Orders and Bookings

Tenants can manage orders, appointments, reservations, deliveries, and fulfillment.

### 7. Dashboards

Business owners and administrators can manage the app, users, content, orders, and settings through dashboards.

### 8. Marketplace

Users can discover templates, components, themes, plugins, integrations, and AI agents to extend their app.

### 9. Mobile and Web

Each tenant app is available on the web and through a mobile app.

### 10. Analytics

Tenants can view usage, sales, engagement, and operational metrics.
