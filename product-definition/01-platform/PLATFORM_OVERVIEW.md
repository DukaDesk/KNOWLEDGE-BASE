# Platform Overview

## What is DUKADESK?

DUKADESK is a no-code platform that allows anyone to create, publish, and operate a digital app for their organization or business.

A single DUKADESK tenant represents one app. That app can serve customers, members, students, staff, or any audience the tenant owner chooses.

## Core Idea

Every organization needs a digital presence. DUKADESK gives them a builder to design their app, dashboards to manage it, and commerce tools to transact with their audience — without writing code.

## What Problems Does DUKADESK Solve?

- Building an app requires developers, designers, and time.
- Existing tools are either too simple or too complex.
- Organizations struggle to manage content, users, orders, and communication in one place.
- Mobile and web presence are managed separately.

## How DUKADESK Solves Them

- A visual builder creates both web and mobile experiences from one design.
- Built-in identity, commerce, orders, media, and notifications reduce the need for integrations.
- A single platform connects the public app, admin dashboards, and owner tools.
- The marketplace helps tenants discover templates, components, and extensions.

## Who Is DUKADESK For?

Small and medium organizations such as:

- Churches
- Schools
- Local businesses
- Service providers
- Community organizations
- Clubs and associations

## What Can a Tenant App Be?

A tenant app can be:

- A storefront
- A school portal
- A church app
- A booking system
- A membership platform
- A content hub
- An event platform

DUKADESK is intentionally domain-agnostic. The builder and modules allow each tenant to shape the app to their needs.
