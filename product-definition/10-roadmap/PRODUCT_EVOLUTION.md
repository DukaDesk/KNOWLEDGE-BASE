# Product Evolution

## Evolution Stages

```text
Foundation
    ↓
Core Product
    ↓
Ecosystem
    ↓
Intelligence
    ↓
Scale
```

## Stage 1: Foundation

**Focus**: Identity, tenant management, builder, dashboards.

**Outcome**: A working platform where a user can sign up, create a tenant, build a simple app, and publish it.

## Stage 2: Core Product

**Focus**: Commerce, orders, payments, notifications, mobile app.

**Outcome**: Tenants can run real businesses or organizations through their app.

## Stage 3: Ecosystem

**Focus**: Marketplace, SDK, CLI, third-party integrations.

**Outcome**: Developers and designers can extend DUKADESK, and tenants can discover extensions.

## Stage 4: Intelligence

**Focus**: AI platform, analytics, automation.

**Outcome**: Tenants receive insights, recommendations, and automated workflows.

## Stage 5: Scale

**Focus**: Enterprise features, performance, white-label, global scale.

**Outcome**: DUKADESK serves large organizations and high-volume tenants.

## Product Evolution Principles

1. Each stage builds on the previous one.
2. New capabilities are driven by user needs and market feedback.
3. The platform remains no-code-first while enabling code when needed.
4. Marketplace and extensibility grow as the user base grows.
