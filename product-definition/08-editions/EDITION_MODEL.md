# Edition Model

## Edition Tiers

```text
Community
    ↓
Starter
    ↓
Professional
    ↓
Enterprise
```

## Editions

### Community

- Free or low-cost tier
- Limited tenants and users
- Core builder and content features
- Community support
- Marketplace access to free items

### Starter

- For small organizations
- One tenant
- Full core features
- Basic commerce
- Email support

### Professional

- For growing organizations
- Multiple tenants
- Advanced commerce and analytics
- Custom domain
- Priority support
- Marketplace paid items

### Enterprise

- For large organizations
- Unlimited tenants
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated support
- SLA
- White-label options (future)

## Edition Comparison

| Capability | Community | Starter | Professional | Enterprise |
|------------|:---------:|:-------:|:------------:|:----------:|
| Tenants | 1 | 1 | Multiple | Unlimited |
| Users | Limited | Limited | More | Unlimited |
| Commerce | No | Basic | Advanced | Advanced |
| Analytics | Basic | Basic | Advanced | Custom |
| Custom Domain | No | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Marketplace | Free only | Free only | Paid | Paid + Custom |
| Support | Community | Email | Priority | Dedicated |

## Edition Flexibility

Specific feature-to-edition mappings are intentionally flexible. They are finalized when pricing and packaging are defined.
