## Mission Statement

**DUKADESK exists to make sophisticated business software accessible to every organization — without forcing them to build it, buy it, or bend it.**

We do this by providing a composable, server-driven, multi-tenant platform that lets businesses design, deploy, and operate their own digital applications through configuration, extension, and marketplace capabilities — not custom engineering.

---

## What We Solve

Organizations today face a false choice:

- **Buy rigid software** that does not fit their business.
- **Build custom software** that is expensive, slow, and hard to maintain.
- **Adapt around broken tools** that create operational debt.

DUKADESK removes this false choice by delivering a platform that is:

- **Powerful enough** for complex business operations.
- **Configurable enough** to match unique workflows.
- **Extensible enough** to grow with the business.
- **Simple enough** to be owned by the business, not a team of engineers.

---

## Who We Serve

DUKADESK serves:

- **Small and medium businesses** that need operational software without enterprise complexity.
- **Industry solution providers** who want to deliver tailored solutions without rebuilding common infrastructure.
- **Enterprise teams** that need tenant isolation, governance, and integration capabilities.
- **Developers and AI agents** who extend the platform through a well-defined, governed ecosystem.

---

## How We Serve Them

DUKADESK delivers value through four pillars:

1. **Builder Studio** — Visual, configuration-driven application design.
2. **Runtime Engine** — Server-driven rendering and execution across surfaces.
3. **Marketplace** — Distribution of capabilities, components, themes, and integrations.
4. **Platform Services** — Identity, data, integration, and enterprise services.

---

## Mission-Driven Constraints

Every DUKADESK decision must be consistent with the mission:

- We do not build features that require every customer to hire engineers.
- We do not optimize for a single tenant at the expense of platform reusability.
- We do not lock customers into surfaces, clouds, or vendors.
- We do not sacrifice long-term maintainability for short-term delivery speed.

---

## Success Measures

The mission is achieved when:

- A business can configure and launch a tailored application without writing code.
- A solution provider can build and distribute an extension through the marketplace.
- A tenant can operate their business on DUKADESK without platform-specific engineering.
- The platform improves for all tenants when any improvement is made.
